Total Recall - RedCarpet Earn Easy Money

Total Recall is RedCarpet's app for earning money for the masses. If RedCarpet is the loan app for the next billion Indians, Total Recall is the app earning money for the next million. Total Recall helps you to create a network of people and allow them to buy the things they want to buy now, pay for them later. This project is the complete overhaul of the app. Changes in the UI and UX took 2 months of research, designing and testing.
